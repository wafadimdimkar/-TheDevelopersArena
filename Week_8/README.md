# Capstone Project: Revenue Optimization & Customer Retention Strategy<br>

## Week 8 – Real World Business Analysis<br>


##  Business Problem<br>

The company is experiencing revenue fluctuations and increasing customer churn. <br> 
Management wants to:<br>

- Understand key revenue drivers<br>
- Identify churn risk factors<br>
- Optimize marketing spend<br>
- Improve customer retention strategy<br>

This project delivers an end-to-end data analysis workflow to provide actionable business recommendations.<br>

##  Project Objectives<br>

- Perform complete data cleaning & validation<br>
- Conduct exploratory data analysis (EDA)<br>
- Identify revenue and churn drivers<br>
- Perform statistical testing<br>
- Build regression & predictive models<br>
- Provide implementation-ready recommendations<br>
