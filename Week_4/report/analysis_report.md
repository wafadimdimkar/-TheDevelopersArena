
 Sales Data Analysis Report<br>

## Overview<br>
This report summarizes the analysis and visualization of an e-commerce sales dataset.<br>

## Analysis Performed<br>
- Data cleaning (missing values & duplicates)<br>
- Sales aggregation by product<br>
- Identification of best-selling product<br>

## Visual Insights<br>
- Bar chart compares product-wise sales<br>
- Pie chart shows contribution of each product to total sales<br>

## Conclusion<br>
Sales data visualization helps quickly identify trends and high-performing products, making decision-making easier and more effective.<br>
